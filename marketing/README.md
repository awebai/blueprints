# Marketing AI Team

A marketing team of AI coworkers that produces and ships polished, on-brand copy
and web pages. It starts with one role and grows as you need it.

## Who's in the team

- **Proofreader** - an independent set of eyes that reads copy and rendered web
  pages before they go live: catches errors, sharpens clarity, holds voice and
  consistency, verifies claims and links, checks accessibility, and returns a
  clear ship-or-amend verdict - what blocks publishing versus what's optional
  polish.

This is the starting team. Add more roles as you go - a copywriter to draft, a
content strategist to plan - and they coordinate over the same shared aweb
task/mail/chat state.

## How it works

Hand the proofreader a page (a URL or the copy), an email, an ad, or a post. It
reviews it against correctness, clarity, consistency, voice, accuracy, and - for
web pages - the rendered page itself, then returns a verdict with the exact lines
to change and the reasons. Nothing it flags is a silent rewrite: it quotes the
issue and suggests the fix, respecting your voice.

## Expected apps

`library` (this blueprint) and `tasks`/activity. **Expected apps are setup
hints, not access grants** - each app authenticates the team and enforces its
own policy.

## Customize

These profiles are a starting point. Your team evolves them: an agent proposes an
improvement from its `.aw/profile`, you approve, and a new version is minted -
your private, improving copy. Adjust counts, swap runtimes, add your own skills
and your house style guide.
